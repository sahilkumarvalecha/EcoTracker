Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer? Contact us

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the homepage. You'll need to provide your email address, create a password, and verify your email to complete registration.

Is EcoTracker free to use?

Yes, EcoTracker is completely free for community members. Our platform is supported by grants and donations to promote environmental awareness.

How do I report an environmental issue?

Navigate to the EcoAlerts tab in the Community Hub. Click "Post Alert" and fill out the form with details about the issue, including location, category, and photos if available.

What happens after I submit a report?

Your report enters our verification queue. Our team reviews each submission, and verified reports are forwarded to the appropriate authorities. You can track progress in your dashboard.

How do I join community discussions?

Go to the Community Hub and select the Discussions tab. Choose an area of interest and participate in existing conversations or start new ones.

Can I organize cleanup events through EcoTracker?

Absolutely! Use the Events section to create and promote environmental activities. You can invite community members and track participation.

The map isn't loading properly. What should I do?

First, try refreshing your browser. If the issue persists, clear your browser cache or try accessing from a different browser. Contact support if the problem continues.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password. The link expires in 24 hours for security.
